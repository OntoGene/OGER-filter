Dataset split for training/testing
==================================

The 67 publically available articles of the CRAFT corpus are split as follows:

  20 test-set.ids
  47 training-set.ids

The tuning purposes, the training set is further split as follows:

  16 dev-set.ids
  31 train-tuning-set.ids

All files are plain-text lists of PubMed IDs.
